Data story idea 1: 'headline' What is the project idea? The idea is to explore data on motor vehicle crashes in NYC in the time period 2014-present. We will explore the temporal patterns, location patterns and historic patterns. Has the traffic safety in NYC increased or decreased since 2014? Are there specific times and locations where motor crashes happen more often? Why? What type of traffic-travellers are most usually killed in these accidents? Pedestrians, cyclists or motorists? Which datasets do you need to explore the idea? Data from NYC 2014-present. 2.25M rows, 29 columns. https://data.cityofnewyork.us/Public-Safety/Motor-Vehicle-Collisions-Crashes/h9gi-nx95/about_data Why is it interesting? It is interesting because patterns might reveal hot spot locations for car crashes. By localizing such hot spots it would be possible to further investigate why the hotspots are located where they are and possibly come up with a solution to make a certain hotspot less prone to car crashes, fx better traffic lights, speed bumps, etc. Regarding the historic development it is interesting to see if the same locations have been prone to car crashes through the history and if so (or not so), why?
